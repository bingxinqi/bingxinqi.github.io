About Me ;)

I am Bingxin Qi and I am a 2nd year PhD student at the University of Denver. I received my Bachelor's degree from Sichuan University and my Master's degree from the University of Pennsylvania. My reearch focuses on minority culture conservation, linguistics and language acquisition, cyber learning. cloud education, and data mining. My goals is to utilize high power computing and visualization cyber infrastucture to develop online learning platfrom so that student are able to access high level education (especailly language learning and science learning) online. I am also interested in developing advanced student assesment tools and platforms using data minng and machine learning to efficiently track their performance and make adjustments accordingly. I am also very interested in traveling, writing, and photography.
